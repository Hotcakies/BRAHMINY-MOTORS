The Future of Mobility: Hydrogen-Electric Hybrid

At the heart of every Brahminy H2X model lies a revolutionary dual-source system: electricity and water. The car uses high-capacity solid-state batteries to perform onboard electrolysis, splitting water into hydrogen and oxygen.

The extracted hydrogen gas feeds a next-gen fuel cell stack, producing clean electricity that powers the motor with zero emissions. The process is self-contained and optimized by our proprietary H2GO Tech™ control system.

Waste heat is recycled to warm the cabin or improve battery efficiency. This system delivers both performance and sustainability—without compromise.
